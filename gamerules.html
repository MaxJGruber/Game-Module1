HOW TO PLAY

  • Hit the matching fret buttons with your keys!
  • A for Green, S for Orange, D for Red, F for Blue
  • For every correct note you hit, you score points!
  • The more successive notes you hit, the bigger your streak!
  • The bigger the streak, the higher your points multiply!
  • Your Goal: TO RELEASE THE ROCKSTAR WITHIN YOU!
